Hi, I'm Oscar.
I love untangling tricky problems.

I get a kick out of exploring how things work, from tech and systems to the stories we tell. My goal? Finding clear, elegant ways forward, even when things seem messy.

Learn More About Me

Driven by Curiosity

It's not just about *how* things function, but the *why* behind them. That's what truly fascinates me and pushes me to make things better.

A Knack for Problem-Solving

I don't see challenges as dead ends; they're more like puzzles inviting a fresh look. I like to dig deep, try out different ideas, and keep asking questions until the simplest, most effective solution clicks into place. It’s like patiently untangling a knot – satisfying when you find the way.

Whether it's refining code, shaping a strategy, or designing a product, my aim is always to turn the complex into something that just *works*, beautifully.

Thinking Through Writing

For me, writing is thinking. It's how I explore different angles, make sense of complex ideas, and share them clearly. I believe a well-put thought can be as satisfying as a perfect piece of code.

I'm less about just listing facts and more about sharing insights that spark a new thought or inspire action. I'm fascinated by the stories that frame our world and how we can build better ones.

How I Like to Work

A few things that guide my thinking and doing:

Go Deep

To really get it, you have to look past the surface. I believe in getting a solid grasp of the core elements before building anything on top.

Build, Learn, Repeat

The best ideas often come from trying things out, seeing what happens, and tweaking as you go. I'm all for this kind of iterative learning and improvement.

Connect with Purpose

Tech and ideas are at their best when they bring people together and add real value. My aim is to create things that are useful, yes, but also thoughtful and human.

Want to Chat

I'm always up for discussing interesting ideas, new challenges, or how we might work together.
